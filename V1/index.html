Welcome to Paradise!

Paradise is a coastal island rich with nature. It may be small, but Paradise has many features including flowing rivers, sandy beaches, shopping and housing districts, and an international airport. Many kind faces have been moving to Paradise in hopes of creating a bright future for themselves.

On this website, you can find all sorts of information of what you can expect from visiting this island. There are many attractions and events that will keep you busy during your stay. The history page will brief you on Paradise's history. You can even find a list of notable members of the community by visiting the residents page.

We hope you enjoy your stay!
